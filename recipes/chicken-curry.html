Chicken Curry

Description:

Indian Chicken Curry is a creamy, aromatic dish with chicken stewed in a tomato-based sauce, flavored with spices like curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, and ginger. The recipe involves sautéing onion and garlic, adding spices, then chicken, tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk. It’s simmered and finished with lemon juice and cayenne pepper, best served with basmati or jasmine rice and naan. This quick and easy recipe can be prepared in under an hour, perfect for a restaurant-quality meal at home

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Curry Powder
  • Cinnamon
  • Paprika
  • Bay leaf
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Tomato Paste
  • Yogurt
  • Coconut Milk
  • Lemon Juice
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps:

  1. Sauté onion and garlic
  2. Add curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, and ginger.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk.
  5. Simmer until chicken is cooked through.
  6. Finish with lemon juice and cayenne pepper.
