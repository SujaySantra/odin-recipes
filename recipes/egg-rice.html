Egg Fried Rice

Description:

This quick and easy fried rice recipe features cooked rice, beaten eggs, carrots, peas, and a hint of soy sauce. It’s designed to taste just like your favorite takeout fried rice

Ingredients:

  • Cooked Rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Soy Sauce

Steps:

  1. Use 4-5 cups of cooked and chilled long-grain rice.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, melt 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat. Add 3 lightly beaten eggs and scramble until fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of butter to the same pan. Sauté 1 diced onion until translucent, then add 3 minced garlic cloves and 1 diced carrot. Cook until tender.
  4. Add the chilled rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps.
  5. Stir in 1 cup of frozen peas and 4 chopped green onions.
  6. Add ¼ cup soy sauce, 2 teaspoons sesame oil, 3 teaspoons oyster sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Optionally, add 1 tablespoon rice vinegar and ½ teaspoon chili flakes.
  7. Return the cooked eggs to the pan and mix everything together until well combined and heated through.
