Paneer Tikka Masala

Description:

This vegetarian dish features cubes of paneer cheese cooked in a spicy, creamy curry sauce. The recipe includes ingredients like onions, bell peppers, jalapeños, ground cashews, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices such as cayenne pepper, cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The sauce is made with tomato sauce and half-and-half, simmered until thickened. This dish pairs well with naan or basmati rice and is perfect for a flavorful meal

Ingredients:

  • Paneer Cheese
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Jalapeño
  • Ground Cashews
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Garam Masala
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Half-and-Half

Steps:

  1. Melt ¼ cup butter in a skillet.
  2. Cook 1 lb paneer cubes until golden.
  3. Add 2 onions, 1 bell pepper, 2 jalapeños, 1 tbsp ground cashews, 1 tsp each of garlic paste, ginger paste, cayenne pepper, cumin, coriander, garam masala. Cook 1 min.
  4. Mix in 16 oz tomato sauce, 1 pint half-and-half, 1 tsp salt.
  5. Simmer until thickened, about 30 mins.
